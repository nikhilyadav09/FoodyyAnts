Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend reservations especially during weekends and holidays to ensure you get a table without waiting. You can make a reservation online or by calling us.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery within a 5km radius. You can order through our website or popular food delivery apps. We also offer takeaway services.

Do you cater for special dietary requirements?

Absolutely! We cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform us of your requirements when making a reservation or ordering.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for corporate events, weddings, parties, and other special occasions. Please contact our events team for customized menus and quotes.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While formal attire is not required, we appreciate guests dressing appropriately for a fine dining experience.
